Welcome to Character Generator D&D 5, an app that allow you to generate a character with random background, race, class and stats. Rules are easy: this app simulates the throw of 4 D6s and then add results of the 3 better ones. It does this for every statistic and in order: STR, DEX, CON, INT, WIS, CHAR. You can exchange 1 statistic with another one only 1 time. If you cheat i will know. Remember informatic is the true Magic. To start just press the botton below.
